Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Spark ("the App"), operated by Kristoffer Knape ("we", "us"), collects and uses your personal data. We are based in Sweden, and the App is governed by the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

If you have questions about this policy or how we handle your data, contact us at spark.support.app@gmail.com.

What we collect

To use Spark you create an account and a profile. We collect:

  • Account data: email address and an authentication identifier provided by Firebase Authentication.
  • Profile data: your name and profile photo.
  • Activity data: the questions you answer, the matches and connections you make, and the messages and images you send through chat.

We do not collect your precise location. We do not access your contacts. We do not run analytics or crash reporting at this time. If we add such features in the future, we will update this policy and notify you.

Why we collect it (legal basis under GDPR)

  • Performance of a contract: account, profile, matches, and messages are processed because they are necessary to deliver the App's core functionality.
  • Consent: any optional features that go beyond the above will ask for your consent.

Who we share it with

We use the following third-party processor:

  • Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Cloud Storage (Google): store account data, profile data, messages, and photos.

We do not sell your data. We will only disclose your data to other parties if legally required (for example, in response to a court order) or to protect users from harm.

International transfers

Firebase may store and process your data outside the European Economic Area, including in the United States. These transfers are protected by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission, or equivalent safeguards.

How long we keep your data

We keep your account and profile data for as long as your account exists. You can delete your account at any time from within the App's settings. When you delete your account, we delete your profile, messages, and photos within 30 days.

Your rights under GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten"). You can delete your account directly in the App, or email us.
  • Restrict or object to certain processing.
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw consent at any time, where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, contact spark.support.app@gmail.com. We will respond within one month.

If you believe we are mishandling your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Swedish Authority for Privacy Protection (Integritetsskyddsmyndigheten, IMY) at imy.se.

Security

Your data is stored on Google Cloud (Firebase) infrastructure, encrypted in transit and at rest. We restrict access to authorised personnel. No system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children

Spark is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the App or by email. Continued use of the App after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.
